# Code of Conduct

Be a grown-up. Disagreements are fine; disrespect isn't.

## Enforcement

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this code, please report
it through one of the channels below.

### Reporting channels

- **Private security advisory** on this repository — preferred for sensitive
  conduct or safety concerns.
- **Email the Project Lead** at the address listed in [`CITATION.cff`](./CITATION.cff).
- **Escalation** — if the report involves the Project Lead, or you are
  unsatisfied with the outcome, contact the next most senior maintainer listed
  in [`GOVERNANCE.md`](./GOVERNANCE.md), or open a public discussion if it is
  safe to do so.

### What to include

- Your contact information (you may remain anonymous if you prefer).
- Who was involved, what happened, where, and when.
- Links, screenshots, or any other relevant context.
- Whether you want the report kept confidential.

### Response timeline

- We will **acknowledge receipt within 7 days**.
- We will aim to **resolve the issue within 30 days**. Complex cases may take
  longer; if so, we will share a status update at least every 14 days.
- Interim measures (for example, temporary interaction limits) may be applied
  while an investigation is ongoing.

### Confidentiality

Reports are handled confidentially. Information is shared only with the
Project Lead and relevant maintainers on a need-to-know basis, unless disclosure
is required by law or is necessary to protect the safety of community members.

### Consequences

Anyone asked to stop unacceptable behavior is expected to comply immediately.
Consequences may include a warning, temporary or permanent interaction limits,
removal from maintainer status, or a ban from the project, depending on severity
and repetition.
