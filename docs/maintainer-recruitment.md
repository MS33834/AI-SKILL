## English

Title: AI-SKILL is looking for the first maintainers

Hi everyone,

AI-SKILL is an open-source, community-driven index of AI agent skill repositories. We currently maintain:

- **38 local SKILL.md files** ready to drop into Claude / Codex / Cursor / Continue
- **928+ external repos** indexed by capability, vendor, domain, and star tier

We just finalized our governance model and contribution guidelines, and we are now moving into the community phase. We need more hands, especially **Design / UX**. Founding maintainer openings:

1. **Content / Curation** — review local skill PRs and enforce vendor-neutral generalization standards
2. **Index / Data** — maintain the external repo index, categories, and metadata refresh
3. **Frontend & Tooling** — maintain the frontend, CI, scripts, and performance
4. **Design / UX** — design system, responsive layout, motion, empty states, brand assets (**urgent**)
5. **Security / QA** — security scan rules, test coverage, and broken-link monitoring
6. **Community & Operations** — contributor onboarding, upstream relations, and release notes

See the full task board at [`docs/tasks.md`](tasks.md).

### What we expect

- Genuine interest in AI agents, prompt engineering, or open-source communities
- ~1–2 hours per week for reviews and discussions
- A willingness to collaborate in public and transparently

### What you get

- A seat at the decision table
- Maintainer credit for your resume / social profiles
- Opportunities to connect with upstream projects like Anthropic, OpenAI, LangChain, etc.

### How to apply

Reply to this discussion with:

1. The maintainer area you want
2. Relevant experience (links welcome)
3. One thing you would like to improve

Or just open a PR to show what you can do: fix an issue, review a PR, or submit a skill.

Looking forward to building the most trustworthy AI agent skill index together.

— badhope & the AI-SKILL team
